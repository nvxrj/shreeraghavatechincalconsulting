Electric Hoist

At SRTC, we offer reliable and high-performance electric hoists for lifting operations in a wide range of industrial environments. These hoists are ideal for vertical lifting of heavy materials, integrated with cranes or standalone lifting stations.

Key Features:

  • Compact design with high lifting capacity
  • Electric motor-driven lifting mechanism with robust gear systems
  • Available in single-speed and dual-speed configurations
  • Hook or trolley-mounted options (fixed or traveling)
  • Anti-drop and overload protection systems

Applications:

  • Manufacturing and assembly lines
  • Construction and maintenance projects
  • Warehouses and loading bays
  • Tool rooms and fabrication shops

Benefits:

  • Efficient lifting with minimal manual effort
  • Durable and low-maintenance components
  • Easy integration with cranes and monorails
  • Smooth and safe operation

Every electric hoist we supply is tested for safety, performance, and durability, ensuring seamless operations in your facility. Custom capacities and mounting types are available on request.
