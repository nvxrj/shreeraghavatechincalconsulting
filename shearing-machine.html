Shearing Machine

Our high-precision shearing machines are designed for clean and accurate cutting of sheet metals with minimal deformation. Built for industrial-grade operations, they provide excellent edge quality and high reliability across a wide range of metals and thicknesses.

Key Features:

  • Hydraulic and mechanical models available
  • Heavy-duty structure with adjustable blade clearance
  • Foot pedal or automatic control operation
  • Back gauge for precise length setting
  • Sturdy hold-downs for vibration-free shearing

Applications:

  • Sheet metal fabrication
  • Automobile body manufacturing
  • Panel cutting for enclosures and cabinets
  • Construction and HVAC industries

Benefits:

  • Accurate and burr-free cuts
  • Long blade life with low maintenance
  • Energy-efficient operation
  • Compact design with high safety standards

With our shearing machines, you can rely on productivity, precision, and safety — even in the most demanding sheet processing environments.
