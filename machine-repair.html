Machine Repair Services

At SRTC, we not only build industry-grade machinery, but also ensure their long-term performance through expert repair and maintenance services. With over two decades of experience, our team specializes in diagnosing, overhauling, and revamping industrial machines of all types and scales.

What We Repair:

  • EOT Cranes and Hoists
  • Slitting Machines and CTL Machines
  • Hydraulic Presses and Shearing Equipment
  • Conveyor Systems and Industrial Automation Lines
  • Gearboxes, Motors, and Control Panels

Our Capabilities:

  • On-site and off-site repair options
  • Fault diagnosis with advanced testing tools
  • Retrofitting and control panel upgrades
  • Preventive maintenance and AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract)
  • Spare part replacement and fabrication

Why Choose Our Repair Services?

  • Quick turnaround with minimal downtime
  • Certified technicians and engineers
  • Genuine replacement parts
  • Detailed reports and transparent process
  • Competitive pricing with reliable support

Whether it's an emergency breakdown or a routine inspection, SRTC is equipped to restore your machinery to peak performance — quickly and efficiently.
