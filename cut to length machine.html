Cut to Length Machine

Our Cut-to-Length (CTL) machines are built to deliver precise sheet cutting from metal coils, providing high-speed performance and clean edges without deformation. Whether you need aluminum, stainless steel, or mild steel sheets, our machines ensure accuracy and versatility.

Core Features:

  • Automatic feeding, straightening, and cutting
  • Programmable logic control for desired lengths and batch sizes
  • Heavy-duty rollers with anti-rust coating
  • High-accuracy servo motor feeding systems
  • Adjustable knife gap for various thickness ranges

Typical Applications:

  • Sheet metal fabrication
  • HVAC ducting and electrical panel making
  • Appliance and enclosure manufacturing
  • Construction and roofing

Benefits:

  • Reduced material waste with exact cuts
  • Rapid operation for increased production
  • Minimal operator involvement with automated controls
  • Custom configurations available for sheet width and thickness

Every SRTC CTL machine is engineered with durability and operational efficiency in mind. We combine precision mechanics and smart automation to support high-volume manufacturing.
